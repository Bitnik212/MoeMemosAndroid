## What's Changed
- Added support for Memos 0.22.0. Versions 0.15 to 0.21 are still supported.
- Added beta support for multiple Memos instances or accounts and switching between them.
- Added support for drag and drop on certain Android devices by @fightyz.
- Introduced initial support for tablet layout.
- Enabled the deletion of memos without the need to archive them first.
