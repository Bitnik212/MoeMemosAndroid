## What's Changed
- Added support for Memos 0.22.1 and 0.22.2. Versions 0.15 to 0.21 are still supported.
- Added beta support for multiple Memos instances or accounts and switching between them.
- Added support for drag and drop on certain Android devices by @fightyz.
- Introduced initial support for tablet layout.
- Enabled the deletion of memos without the need to archive them first.

## New Contributors
* @fightyz made their first contribution in https://github.com/mudkipme/MoeMemosAndroid/pull/166

## Changes since 0.8.0
- Supports Memos 0.22.1 and 0.22.2. Please upgrade to 0.22.1 or higher if you are using 0.22.0.
- Fixed the issue where tags were not created when using Memos 0.21 or lower.
- Fixed the issue where the username wasn't displayed on the Explore page.
